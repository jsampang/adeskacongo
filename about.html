About ADESKACONGO

ADESKA is a non-profit organization whose objective is to obtain and ship medical equipment and medicine to the Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC) to help villagers victims of persistant political chaos, unending civil wars, and consequent abject poverty in Kabambare province in Eastern DRC.

Appalling United Nations statistics of health indicators about the DRC are a testimony of the very bad health situation in the country, but those statistics are reflective of major cities only while the vast rural areas remain unaccounted for. In those rural areas which by the way constitute the vast majority of the country geographical territory, people do not have access to even the very basic healthcare facility.
